    Severe weather strikes East Idaho

    • Doug Iverson

      Doug Iverson

    • May 24, 2023

    Tuesday was a wild weather day across East Idaho. Thunderstorms produced flash flooding in Idaho Falls, Blackfoot, and Rexburg. Torrential rain and hail caused a roof to collapse south of Idaho Falls. Quarter to golf ball size hail was reported in the Pocatello area along with wind gusts over 60 mph. Looks like there's a risk of more severe thunderstorms in the afternoon/evening hours on Wednesday.
