Parts of Minnesota to be impacted by late season winter storm

(Undated)--The National Weather Service in Chanhassen says they are continuing to monitor a late-week winter storm that will provide potential impacts to the state of Minnesota in the form of both rain and snow. It looks now like the heaviest snow will track off off to our east on Thursday. Portions of east central Minnesota into Wisconsin could see more than 6 inches of snow with the system.
