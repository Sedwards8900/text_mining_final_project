More Storms Roll Through Maryland, Tornado Reported In Glenelg

Print
Thursday, May 30, 2019
WBAL-TV 11 News and WBAL NewsRadio 1090/FM 101.5

  • Storm clouds over Howard County Credit: Courtesy Michelle Dobbs

More severe storms are moving through Maryland Thursday afternoon. A severe thunderstorm watch has been issued for much of Maryland until 9 p.m., but it was canceled west of the Chesapeake Bay after the late afternoon.

LATEST CONDITIONS

A tornado warning issued for Anne Arundel and Howard counties expired at 3:45 p.m. The National Weather Service later confirmed a tornado touched down in Glenelg at 2:22 p.m. The weather service will conduct a survey Friday to determine the extent of damage and the strength of the tornado.

A tornado warning issued for Frederick County was canceled shortly before 3 p.m.
