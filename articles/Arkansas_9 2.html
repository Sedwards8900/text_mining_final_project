Arkansas Storm Team Blog: Severe t-storm watch for SW Arkansas until 5 pm

by: Alex Libby

Posted: Jun 10, 2023 / 01:00 PM CDT

Updated: Jun 10, 2023 / 01:05 PM CDT

by: Alex Libby

Posted: Jun 10, 2023 / 01:00 PM CDT

Updated: Jun 10, 2023 / 01:05 PM CDT

The storm prediction center has issued a severe thunderstorm watch for several counties in southwest Arkansas until 5 pm.

Locations under the watch could see damaging wind gusts over 60 mph. Minor damage and power outages will be possible/

The rest of Arkansas will see thunderstorms, but the risk for damaging winds is lower. Small hail could be seen in some of the stronger thunderstorms. Tornadoes are not likely Saturday.

We will see another chance for strong to severe thunderstorms Sunday afternoon. Stick with the Arkansas Storm Team for continued updates on this weekend’s storm chances.

Download the Arkansas Storm Team app 

To make sure you are staying up-to-date with the forecast, download the Arkansas Storm Team app to get updates anywhere at any time.
