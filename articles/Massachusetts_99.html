by: Storyful, Mekaela Muck

A bolt of lightning struck down onto the surface of the Charles River in Massachusetts on Wednesday, June 14.

Footage recorded by Juan Pablo Ugarte shows the near-miss lightning strike near the Western Avenue Bridge.

Both Boston and Cambridge were under Severe Thunderstorm Warning on Wednesday evening, the National Weather Service said.
