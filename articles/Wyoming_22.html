Section 40-12-704 - Disclosure Requirements for Exterior Storm Damage Repair Contracts.

Universal Citation: WY Stat § 40-12-704 (2019)

40-12-704. Disclosure requirements for exterior storm damage repair contracts.

Any contract for exterior storm damage repairs shall include a copy of a repair proposal that contains the disclosures required under W.S. 40-12-703(a).
