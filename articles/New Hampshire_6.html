Active weather will return to the region on Thursday as a cold
front brings showers and thunderstorms with the potential for
flooding and severe weather. This front will weaken as it moves
into the Gulf of Maine with a hot and humid day expected on
Friday. Another cold front will move through the region on
Saturday bringing a renewed round of showers and thunderstorms.
Much cooler and drier air is expected to arrive across the
region Saturday night continuing through early next week.

1020 PM EDT Wed Jul 26 2023
