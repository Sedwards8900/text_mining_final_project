Storm causes major wash out on Oklahoma City bridge

Summary by Ground News
Heavy rain during weekend storms cause bridge in Oklahoma City to collapse. Sunday storms left behind quite a bit of damage throughout the metro, from Norman to Oklahoma City. The storms cause a chunk of Britton Road to fall into the Canadian River. A team of engineers came out on Monday to survey the partial collapse.
