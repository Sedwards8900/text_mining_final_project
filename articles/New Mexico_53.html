Weather Conditions on I-10 in New Mexico

New Mexico along Interstate I-10 experiences a wide variety of weather throughout the year.

In the summer months, temperatures can reach the 90s and even 100s during the day and drop back into the 70s and 80s at night. The area also experiences monsoon season in July and August, with thunderstorms and heavy rain.

Winters can be quite cold and dry. Temperatures can drop into the 20s and 30s and snowfall possible, especially in the higher elevations. Spring and fall are usually mild, with temperatures ranging from the 50s to 80s.

No matter what time of year you travel through New Mexico along I-10, bring warm and cool weather clothing so that you can be prepared for any type of weather.
