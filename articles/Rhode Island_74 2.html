      Ice hockey team in Kingston, Rhode Island
      Rhode Island Storm
      CityKingston, Rhode Island
      LeagueNorth East Professional Hockey League
      Founded2009
      Home arenaBradford R. Boss Ice Arena
      ColorsRed, White, Blue
      Owner(s)Ed McDonough

      Franchise history
      2009–2010Rhode Island Storm

      The Rhode Island Storm were a professional ice hockey team in the North East Professional Hockey League that played in the 2009–10 season. They played their home games at the Bradford R. Boss Ice Arena on the Kingston campus of the University of Rhode Island.[1][2]

      2009–10 19 8 10 1 17 2nd of 3, NEPHL Lost Championship Series, 0–2 vs. New York Aviators
