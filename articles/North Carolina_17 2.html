Photos: A tornado ripped through part of eastern North Carolina, causing damage

By Elizabeth Townsend and Jennifer Gamertsfelder Coastal
UPDATED 8:30 AM ET Jul. 20, 2023 PUBLISHED 2:44 PM ET Jul. 19, 2023 PUBLISHED 2:44 PM EDT Jul. 19, 2023

A tornado spun up Wednesday as storms tracked across the state, and the twister hit Nash County and nearby areas, causing damage in its wake.

According to the NWS storm survey, 16 people were hurt.
