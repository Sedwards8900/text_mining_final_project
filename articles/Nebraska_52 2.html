Tornadoes leave destruction across eastern Nebraska

Friday night's storms left damage across Dodge County
By Johan Marin
Published: May. 13, 2023 at 6:12 PM CDT

DODGE COUNTY, Neb. (WOWT) - Friday night’s storms left damage in several parts of Nebraska, including Dodge County.

About 40 miles from the Omaha metro, the National Weather Service is out in Dodge County and other areas surveying the damage.

We are sending out three storm survey teams today to look at the damage from the tornadoes on Friday, May 12, 2023. We hope to have some preliminary results later this evening. pic.twitter.com/LMrFjOrmiJ

— NWS Omaha (@NWSOmaha) May 13, 2023
RELATED: Severe weather impacts eastern Nebraska counties, destroys pig farm

Fallen trees, destroyed homes and barns and other destruction can be seen in several areas across Dodge County.

The storm also left many without power. Earlier Saturday OPPD was working to get it restored. At last check Saturday evening, all affected customers in Dodge County should have had power restored.

This is a developing story. Stay with 6 News for updates.
